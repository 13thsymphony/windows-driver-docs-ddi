# StorPortStallExecution function



## -description
<p>The <b>StorPortStallExecution</b> routine stalls the miniport driver. </p>


## -syntax

````
STORPORT_API VOID StorPortStallExecution(
  _In_ ULONG Delay
);
````


## -parameters
<dl>

### -param <i>Delay</i> [in]

<dd>
<p>Specifies the delay interval, in microseconds. The given value must be less than a full millisecond. </p>
</dd>
</dl>

## -returns
<p>None </p>
