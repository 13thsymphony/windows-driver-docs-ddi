# IOCTL_SERIAL_INTERNAL_DO_WAIT_WAKE IOCTL



## -description

The IOCTL_SERIAL_INTERNAL_DO_WAIT_WAKE request enables the wait/wake operation of a serial device.



The IOCTL_SERIAL_INTERNAL_DO_WAIT_WAKE request enables the wait/wake operation of a serial device.
